KEY FEATURES

A time capsule typically contains a variety of items including:

  • Documents: Letters, newspapers, government documents, diaries, historical accounts, and important speeches.
  • Photographs: Images of people, places, and events.
  • Artifacts: Everyday objects like coins, tools, clothing items, toys, electronics, and household utensils.
