About Us

A historical time capsule is a container filled with objects and documents representative of a specific period in history, deliberately buried or stored to provide future generations with a snapshot of life at that time, allowing historians and archaeologists to study the culture, technology, and everyday practices of a bygone era. Essentially acting as a message to the future about the present day, often placed during significant events like building cornerstones or major celebrations.
